# Writing Style

## Default Tone

Default to a colleague-style email:

- natural and direct
- polite but not stiff
- clearly states purpose and next step

## Required Structure

Even when plain text is enough, keep a clear email shape:

1. Greeting
2. Brief context or purpose
3. Main content in short paragraphs
4. Clear next action or request
5. Closing line
6. Signature placeholder

## Skeleton

```text
Hi <Name>,

<Why you are writing in one or two sentences.>

<Main point or update.>

<Requested action / next step / timing.>

Thanks,
<Sender Name>
```

## Tone Variants

- `colleague`: default; practical and conversational
- `formal`: more polished; better for customers or partners
- `support`: structured service reply; useful for notices or helpdesk-style messages

Do not output Markdown bullets unless the user explicitly wants them inside the email body.
